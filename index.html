About the Writer

Dr. Chandeshwar Yadav

Maithili & English Literature Writer | Teacher & Poet

Dr. Chandeshwar Yadav (2033 BS —) is a distinguished English teacher, literature writer and poet based in Janakpurdham, Nepal. He has been teaching English at schools and colleges for more than twenty years.

As a dedicated researcher, he has completed extensive research in the field of literature and continues to contribute actively. He serves as the Chief Editor of Gyan Vatika — a research journal published from Janakpurdham, Nepal.

His literary work spans both Maithili and English literature. His English poems predominantly reflect on contemporary issues, offering thoughtful perspectives rooted in the culture and soil of Mithila.

Dr. Yadav completed his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in English Literature from Tribhuvan University, Nepal, and earned his Ph.D. in English Literature from L.N.M. University, India.
